NATIONAL SCIENCE & MATHEMATICS DAY'24 CELEBRATIONS

National Science Day, observed on February 28th, commemorates the discovery of the Raman Effect by Indian physicist Sir C.V. Raman in 1928. It serves as a tribute to scientific innovation and discovery, highlighting the transformative power of curiosity and exploration.
